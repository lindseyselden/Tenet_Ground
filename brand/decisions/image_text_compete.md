## What changed
The scaffold's notes now state that image references override text on whatever attributes they carry
most strongly — including unintended secondary qualities — and that references should be used only
when they fully embody the target, with style weight lowered aggressively or removed when they
contaminate.

## Why
Iterating with a previous "good" ring as style reference amplified secondary qualities of that
reference (a hint of gloss, gallery environment, polite plinth) that the text prompt was actively
fighting. Image references and text prompts do not combine additively — they compete, and the
dominant signal wins on whatever attribute it carries most strongly.

## What this supersedes
Nothing — establishes the reference-handling guidance, and seeds the backlog item for a vetted
anchor-reference library coded by the register each image carries.
