# Lexicon

The native vocabulary of Tenet Ground, the `Ground [X]` naming system, and the words to use and
avoid. The voice is future-fluent ([`voice.md`](voice.md)): these terms are used as established fact,
never explained or hedged.

## Index of terms

**Ground Truth Infrastructure.** The foundational data layer that continuously verifies the provable
reality of a transaction, independent of what is presented on the surface. *In 2035 you don't trust
the email or the invoice; you trust the Ground Truth.*

**Behavioral Whitelist.** An evolving, AI-native registry of legitimate intent. Not a static list of
names — it catalogs the rhythmic pulse of how a specific vendor or partner actually operates. *If a
transaction does not match the Behavioral Whitelist, it's not viable.*

**Digital Twin Deception.** A high-frequency attack where an AI perfectly replicates the voice,
writing style, and behavioral history of a trusted executive or vendor. *The primary threat the
Integrity Architect is trained to neutralize.*

**High-Frequency Deception (HFD).** Fraudulent strikes executed at machine speed, designed to exploit
the milliseconds between a transaction's initiation and its settlement. *The 2035 successor to the
slow-motion business email compromise of the 2020s.*

**Transactional Immunity.** A state of systemic health in which a business is no longer defending
against attacks but is architecturally incapable of processing unverified intent. *We moved beyond
"security" to reach "immunity."*

**Integrity Architect.** The professional successor to the CFO and CISO. (See [`audience.md`](audience.md).)

## The `Ground [X]` naming system

Named elements tie to the brand name and its core concept:

- **Ground Truth** — the principle (the foundational verified reality).
- **Ground Ring** — the personal instrument (worn by the Architect). See
  [`visual/ground-ring.md`](visual/ground-ring.md).
- **Ground Hall** — the architectural space (the threshold). See
  [`visual/ground-hall.md`](visual/ground-hall.md).
- **Ground Standard** — possible future: a certification mark or industry-compliance designation.

The pattern is extensible. As the brand world grows, evaluate each new name for whether `Ground [X]`
serves it — and whether it preserves precision or becomes a tic. The pattern is a tool, not a
mandate; when it stops earning its place, stop using it. (`name-forge` enforces this judgment.)

## Words we use

Ground truth, intent, behavioral, verify, immunity, infrastructure, recessive, machine speed,
intent-loop, collapse the gap, signal, evidence, certainty.

## Words we avoid

Disruptive, revolutionary, next-gen, cutting-edge, amazing, game-changing, solutions, seamless
(as filler), empower, unlock (as hype), "stop fraud fast," any exclamation-pointed urgency. See
[`anti-patterns.md`](anti-patterns.md) for the visual and territorial equivalents.
