# Messaging

The pillars copy ladders up to, the approved lines, and the boilerplate. Generation skills anchor an
idea to a pillar; `copy-reviewer` checks that copy serves one.

## The hero standard

**Intent. Verified.** The two-word standard for the 2035 economy. If it isn't verified by Tenet
Ground, it isn't viable.

## Pillars

**1. Seeing is no longer believing.** In the age of the Digital Twin, the surface is a liability.
The threat looks, talks, and thinks like your vendor. The pillar that frames the problem.

**2. Verify intent, not documents.** We don't audit paper; we read the behavioral ground truth.
Intent is the signal; behavior is evidence. The pillar that frames the method.

**3. The human is the last ground truth.** Behind machine-speed value transfer is a person whose
behavior can't be faked. The system verifies the human in the loop — it doesn't bypass them —
because the Integrity Architect's own signature is the one thing a deepfake can't reproduce. The
pillar that frames the human stakes.

**4. Immunity, not defense.** The goal isn't to fight off attacks but to become architecturally
incapable of processing unverified intent. The pillar that frames the outcome.

## Approved lines

- **Hero:** *Intent. Verified.*
- **Manifesto close:** *One system. One intent. Tenet Ground.*
- **Method line:** *The intent is verified. The transaction is clear.*
- **Thesis line:** *We collapse the gap between numbers and nerves.*

Use these as written. New lines are generated by `brand-writer` against [`voice.md`](voice.md) and
the matching surface spec, then checked by `copy-reviewer`.
