# Treatment

The photographic register — how every image is captured, lit, and felt. These are the scaffold's
locked OPTICS, LIGHTING, and MOOD tokens, stated as the canonical treatment so skills and reviewers
share one definition.

## Optics

Medium-format photographic register, large negative. Controlled depth of field at f/5.6–f/8,
hyper-sharp focal plane, no bokeh blur. Restrained dynamic range, low-to-medium saturation
throughout, slight cool cast in shadows, neutral midtones. No HDR, no oversharpening, no digital
color grading.

Focal length varies by subject: 100mm macro for object/specimen, 80mm for portrait, 35mm for
architectural interior, 24mm when architecture is the subject.

## Lighting

Single directional daylight at 5600K, hard-edged primary source, soft ambient bounce only. No
artificial fill, no colored gels. Defined shadow with controlled falloff.

Emissive accents, when present: low-intensity warm amber at 2700K, point-source. No bloom, no halo,
no lens flare — it reads as a status indicator on a precision instrument, not as glow. This is the
behavioral line that keeps the brand on the right side of governance Rule #02.

Primary direction varies: raking side (macro), overhead oculus or north clerestory (interior),
skylight shaft (lobby). Hardness varies: raking-hard for macro, diffused-directional for interior.

## Mood

Clinical precision married with living attention — surgical care, not cold examination. Institutional
gravitas with sensate awareness, not warmth. The calm of a surgeon's hands and the patience of a
conservator. "The nerves in the numbers."

**Cold-failure mode** (off-brand): cold, sterile, robotic, antiseptic, lab-clinical, corporate,
detached, mechanical, dystopian, surveillant, lifeless.

**Warm-failure mode** (off-brand): warm, cozy, friendly, approachable, soft, nurturing, gentle,
welcoming, comforting, lifestyle.

The target is the precise middle. These two lists are the *visual* form of the two-sided failure —
its thesis lives in [`../beliefs.md`](../beliefs.md) ("numbers have nerves") and the copy form in
[`../voice.md`](../voice.md). A render can satisfy all three governance rules and still fail here.
